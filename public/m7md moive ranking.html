My Top 3 Movies

The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is a powerful drama about a banker wrongly imprisoned for murder who, over decades in Shawshank prison, forms a deep friendship and ultimately orchestrates a brilliant escape that symbolizes hope, resilience, and redemption.

The Green Mile

The Green Mile is a poignant supernatural drama film set on death row in 1930s Louisiana, where a prison guard discovers that a gentle giant of an inmate possesses miraculous healing powers that challenge his understanding of justice and humanity.

Fast & Furious

Fast & Furious is a high-octane action film franchise that follows Dominic Toretto and his crew as they evolve from street racers into globe-trotting operatives battling crime, all while emphasizing themes of loyalty, family, and adrenaline-fueled adventure.
